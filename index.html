Web Development

Web development involves the entire process of designing, building, and maintaining websites or web applications that are accessible through the internet. Web development is important because humans interact with the web every single day. It is what drives businesses to grow, allows data to move freely around the world and drives the world to becomea better place. As technology continues to advance, the web will be required to adapt, making web development a valuable skill.
